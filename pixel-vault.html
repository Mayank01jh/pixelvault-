Fast, Private, Multilingual Search in Action

Watch Pixel Vault retrieve your electricity bills with a single Hindi voice command — instantly, privately, entirely on your device.

  • 01 Speak your query in Hindi or English — tap the mic and say what you need.
  • 02 Offline STT processes audio on-device. No internet required. No data transmitted.
  • 03 Results surface from across all connected sources, ranked by relevance score.
  • 04 Open, share, or organize documents — your encrypted vault, always in your control.
